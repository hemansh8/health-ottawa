Training Services

The H.E.A.L.T.H clinic is a not for profit primary care clinic located in downtown Ottawa. It is the first of its kind in Canada. Our mission is to provide high quality trauma informed primary care and to train health care professionals to care for populations who have experienced violence.

Established in 2017, we are always innovating to help our patients and the communities we serve stay healthy. Join us - we look forward to meeting you.

Presentations

Whether you are an educator, front line health care provider, policy maker, teacher, allied health provider, etc. We have workshops and presentations available for you on the following topics:

  • Trauma informed health care
  • Reproductive Coercion
  • How Violence Affects Your Health
  • Human Trafficking and the Intersections of Health Care
  • Association of Trauma on Mental Health and Substance Use Disorders
  • Adverse Childhood Experiences and Impacts of Health
